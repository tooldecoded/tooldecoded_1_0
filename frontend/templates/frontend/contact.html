Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the database updated?

We update our database regularly as new products are released and specifications become available. Major updates typically occur monthly, with smaller corrections and additions made continuously.

Can I suggest new products to add?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new products, components, or brands to include in our database. Please email us with as much information as possible, including product names, model numbers, and any specifications you have.

What if I find incorrect information?

We appreciate corrections! Please contact us with the specific product, the incorrect information, and the correct information along with your source. We'll verify and update the database accordingly.

How can I stay updated on new features?

We regularly announce new features and updates through our website. You can also contact us to be added to our update notifications list for major releases and new functionality.
